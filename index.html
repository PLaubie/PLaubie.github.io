I am currently a post-doc researcher at Institut Elie Cartan de Lorraine, UMR 7502 de l'Université de Lorraine under the supervision of Yvain Bruned .

I'm very interested in combinatorics, algebraic operads and homotopy theory and more precisely relationship between those.

I'm currently in the team Probabilités et Statistique . This may incongruous given my research interest, however, Algebraic Operads have proven useful in Regularity Structures which are used to solve SPDs.
